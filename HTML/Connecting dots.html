Connecting the dots

About the game

A game from our childhood's classrooms, connect the dots and win points for every box that you complete. Get ready to feel the nostaglia and play it again, as we present your very own multiplayer game, challenge your friends to play it too.
1. The game starts with a toss
2. Person who wins the toss will make the first move.
3. This move includes drawing a line between x and y so the player enters the coordinates x and y.
NOTE : x can only be such a point from where a line/lines has already been drawn
