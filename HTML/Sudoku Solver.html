Sudoku Solver

About the game

We all are well aware of this daily puzzle that has been mind-boggling at times. After reading the morning newspaper we've spent hours to solve it with utmost indulgence. Have you ever been challeged to solve a sudoku?
This is your very own sudoku solver that can solve any 9x9 sudoku within seconds!!
Instructions :
You need to enter each row of the sudoku with 0s for blank spaces and rest numbers where they occur.
