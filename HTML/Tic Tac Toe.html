Tic Tac Toe

About the game

Tic Tac Toe has been a part and parcel of everyone's childhood. It is a game with no special equipments required just a pen, paper and intuition. Herein we present a computer program ready to compete with you on a game of tic tac toe. Hope you enjoy it!
Instructions :
1. It is a single plyer game that begins with a toss for who will begin first
2. Select a position and make your move against the artificial genius.
