Encryption 101

About the game

Ever wanted to encrpyt your own messages so that no one could read them except the person intended?
We present you, your very own encryptor that can encode and decode messages according to specific logics quite famous in the world of cryptography
Instructions :
1. Select the difficulty level of encryption among the 3 choices.
2. Select whether you wish to encode or decode.
3. Type the message/cipher and key.
